1. Winner Of MPLID-S12


ONIC Esports won the first game of the MPL S12 grand final with an elimination score of 17-5 in a short match lasting around 14 minutes. This success was achieved thanks to the superior performances of heroes Claude from CW, Fredrinn from Kairi, and Gord from Sanz who displayed tactical and aggressive play. As a result, Onic Esports managed to take the lead with a temporary score of 1-0. Onic Esports' superiority continued in the second game, where they dominated by scoring an elimination score of 23-4 in a match that lasted around 19 minutes.


0NIC Esports Roamer, Kiboy, was chosen as the MPL ID S12 Finals MVP after successfully helping his team become champions. In another way, Kiboy played the most significant role in ONIC Esports' success in winning the MPL ID S12 title. However, it is important to remember that the contribution and hard work of other players and the coaching staff also have an important role that cannot be ignored. Kiboy, as a roamer in the six MPL ID S12 grand final matches, played with five different heroes. He used Rafaela twice and once each for several other heroes such as Mathilda, Floryn, Kaja, and Hilda.



2. Winner of Lantam League

RRQ Akira became the best team in Latin America after successfully becoming champion in the LATAM League Season 2 on Monday, June 20 2022. Facing the home team, Malvinas Gaming, in the Grand Final, RRQ Blink and friends succeeded in winning with a score of 4-1 . Not only that, RRQ Akira's Explaner, RRQ Tekashi, won the MVP title in the Grand Final.


The RRQ Akira match against Malvinas Gaming also had the highest peak viewers in the LATAM League Season 2 tournament, reaching 30,494 peak viewers. The match from RRQ Akira apparently attracted the attention of esports fans in the Latin American region. This fact cannot be separated from the data released by Esports Chart. Four of the five matches with the highest peak viewers were matches played by RRQ Akira.

4. Up Coming M world championship



M World Championship, often abbreviated as M, is an annual tournament organized by Moonton. The M World Championship remains the biggest and most prestigious event, and represents the World Championship in each competitive Mobile Legends season.

The first M World Championship for the 2019 Season, known as M1 was held in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, in the Axiata Arena. The tournament would feature 16 teams around the globe, with a portion coming from their respective Professional League.
