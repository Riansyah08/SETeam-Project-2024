1. PMPL ID Fall 2023

Persija Evos mengukir sejarah dalam penyelenggaraan PUBG Mobile Pro League (PMPL) Indonesia Fall 2023 dengan meraih gelar juara setelah mengantongi 11 kali "Winner Winner Chicken Dinner" atau WWDC.

Made Bagas Pramudita atau Zuxxy dan kawan-kawan mengantongi total 529 poin selama dua pekan turnamen yang berlangsung pada 7-18 Juni itu. Dengan raihan poin tersebut, dominasi Persija Evos atas 15 kontestan lainnya di PMPL ID Fall 2023 sangat terasa. Sebab, gap dengan peringkat kedua terpaut lebih dari 100 poin, sedangkan dengan peringkat ke-16 sekitar 400 poin.

CEO Persija Esports Harlin E. Rahardjo bangga dengan pencapaian Persija Evos. Menurut dia, prestasi gemilang pada PMPL ID Fall 2023 harus menjadi momentum untuk mengukir kejayaan selanjutnya bagi Persija Evos atau pun Persija Esports.


2. PUBG Nations Cup 2023

The PUBG Nations Cup 2023 is the third Nations Cup, also represents the All-Star event in the 2023 Season. Top players are chosen by their peers to represent their nation in competitive PUBG. Only the most talented battle royale players have a chance to take on the world's best for a shot at glory and a share of the prize pool.

Prize Pool $300,000 USD are spread among the nations as seen below: An additional 30% of all PNC 2023 Skin sales are added to the total prize pool. The total prize pool after crowdfunding is $710,000 USD

The winner of PUBG Nations Cup 2023 was determined based on the results of 18 rounds in which teams earned rating points for kills and placements. The champion was the South Korean team, represented by players from the Danawa e-sports and Veronica7 teams, who managed to accumulate 172 points and secure 106 kills


3. PUBG Newest patch incoming


Area Modifications We have enhanced areas near the center of the Erangel map that are less frequently explored by players. Stalber and the submerged village have been visually refreshed, Mylta has been given a makeover that retains its essence while offering a fresh perspective, and Ruins and Georgopol have been optimized for smoother gameplay with improved loot distribution and navigation options.

Not only some modification to its map area KFC, renowned for serving the finest Chicken Dinners, has landed in Erangel. Explore KFC stores on the revamped Erangel map and keep an eye out for upcoming KFC events! Erangel's gas stations have transformed into KFC stores. You can obtain exclusive KFC Chicken Combos from kiosks nestled inside KFC stores. Engage with the kiosk using the Interaction Key. After your order is placed, your nickname will appear on the order number display board as your KFC Chicken Combo spawns on the counter. Each player may use the kiosk once per KFC store and once more after entering Phase 4.
